Loaded Potato Soup

Creamy cheesy potato soup loaded with so much flavor. Easy quick dinner to warm your body and home.

Prep time 20mins. Cook time 20 mins. Total time 20 mins.

Ingredients

  • 4 large potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 1//2 tsp salt
  • Bacon cut to bite size pieces (adjust to your preference)
  • 5 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 1/2 onion finely chopped
  • 3 cloves minced garlic
  • 1/4 cup all purpose flour
  • 2 1/2 cups whole milk/li>
  • 2 1/2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup mild cheddar cheese
  • 3/4 cups sour cream
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • green onions for garnish

Steps

  1. Peel and cube potatoes into even pieces. Add the potatoes into a pot and cover with slightly salted water. Cook for about 10 minutes or until potatoes can be pierced through easily. Drain and set aside.
  2. Cube and cut bacon until desired doneness. Save 1 tbsp of bacon grease and set aside.
  3. Cook butter and onions until tender. Add the minced garlic and cook an additional minute. Stir constantly to ensure it doesnt burn.
  4. Slowly whisk in flower until incorporated. Add in the milk and chicken broth and bring to a low boil.
  5. Add the potatoes and season with salt and pepper. At this point you can leave the potatoes whole or use an immersion blender for a creamy texture.
  6. Mix in sour cream, shredded cheese, and bacon. Bring to a boil then remove from heat.
  7. Serve and enjoy!
