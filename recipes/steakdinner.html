Steak Dinner

Papa's Porterhouse Dinner

Papa has perfected the steak dinner with a juicy porterhouse paired with roasted garlic butter potatoes and lemon pepper green beans.

Prep time 30mins. Cook time 30 mins. Serving Size: 2.

Ingredients

For the steak:

  • (2) 2‚Äù-thick Porterhouse steak, trimmed
  • 3 tbsp unsalted butter at room temp
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil, kosher salt, and freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh parsley
  • 2 cloves garlic

For the veggies:

  • 2 lb golden or red potatoes, cubed
  • 1 lb fresh green beans
  • 4 tbsp unsalted butter, divided
  • 1 tbsp salt and pepper or to taste
  • 1/2 tbsp lemon pepper seasoning
  • 1 tbsp chopped fresh parsley
  • 4 tbsp minced garlic, divided
  • (Optional) 1 tsp chili flakes

Steps

  1. Let steak rest outside of fridge for 30 mins. Salt and pepper both sides. In the mean time prepare the sides.
  2. Cube the potatoes and add to a large mixing bowl. Add 2 tbsp melted butter, 2 tbsp garlic, salt, pepper, and parsley (chili flakes optional). Spread onto a lined baking tray and bake for 20 minutes covered at 350 F. Uncover, flip, and bake another 10 minutes.
  3. Wash and cut the tips off the green beans. In a skillet heat oil and remaining butter on medium heat. Add in the green beans and season with lemon pepper and optional chili flakes and cook for 10 minutes stirring constantly. The last five minutes add in garlic and enjoy.
  4. Heat a large enough pan to medium and add oil and butter. For medium rare sear steak for 5 minutes on each side. Remove and let rest 5 minutes before cutting and serving. Garnish with parsley.
